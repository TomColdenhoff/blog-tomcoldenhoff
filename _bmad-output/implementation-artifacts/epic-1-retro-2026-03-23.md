## Challenges and patterns (systems, not blame)

1. **Cross-story dependency clarity** — 1.2 and 1.3 both touch `baseURL` and CI behavior; the stories anticipated this (dev notes on `configure-pages` vs custom domain). **Lesson:** When the next cross-cutting concern appears (Epic 2 content validation vs CI), capture the **decision** in one doc and link from README/workflow.  
2. **Solo pipeline concurrency** — Workflow uses `cancel-in-progress: true`. Fine for one publisher; worth a line in the runbook when triaging “which deploy won?” (Story 1.4 scope).  
3. **Review vs done** — 1.4 in **`review`** means the **definition of done** for Epic 1 is not closed until review outcomes are resolved and status advances.
