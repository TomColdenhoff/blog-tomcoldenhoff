This site is a personal publishing space for **Tom Coldenhoff**. Posts cover entrepreneurship, software, AI, and science-adjacent topics—practical notes, experiments, and longer-form ideas as they take shape.

## What you will find

- Long-form articles and shorter updates, published on a simple static stack (Hugo and the PaperMod theme).
- Content aimed at clarity and usefulness rather than a fixed posting cadence.

## Colophon

Built with [Hugo](https://gohugo.io/) and [PaperMod](https://github.com/adityatelange/hugo-PaperMod). Source and publishing workflow: see the [repository README](https://github.com/TomColdenhoff/blog-tomcoldenhoff/blob/main/README.md) and [publishing checklist](https://github.com/TomColdenhoff/blog-tomcoldenhoff/blob/main/docs/publishing-checklist.md) on GitHub if you are contributing or mirroring the setup.
